Herbaceous Fork

Welcome to Herbaceous Fork. We feature a healthy, vegetable-focused menu. Most of our menu items are plant-based and high in nutrients, with minimal use of dairy and proteins. We are here to showcase local farmers and their vegetables in as many ways as we can imagine.
