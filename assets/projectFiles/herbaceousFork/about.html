Our Story

Herbaceous Fork was created by Chef Michael Smith and his wife and business partner, Veronica in the summer of 2020. Michael and Veronica met in the restaurant business and have many years of experience in the art of hospitality.

In the fall of 2020, the ownership group of the Market at Camden Yards approached Michael and Veronica to join the opening collection of chefs and entrepreneurs. Through numerous tastings and meetings, it was obvious to both sides that this partnership was worth exploring.

We try to create a sense of adventure for our guests by creating exciting combinations of flavor profiles that are unique only to Herbaceous Fork. The feeling of unlocking that one-of-a-kind balance of all these elements fuels us to keep innovating.

After a considerate amount of research into the concept and vision of the group behind The Market at Camden Yards, Michael and Veronica decided to be a part of the opening collection of chefs.

It was the genuine sense of community, curiosity, and collaboration of small businesses that swayed the decision to join the food hall movement with this extremely talented group. We are proud to be part of this team and we are extremely excited to share this experience with the twin cities.
